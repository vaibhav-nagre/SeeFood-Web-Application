Our Story


The idea for SEEFOOD came a few years ago when I was out-of-town visiting a friend in Chico, CA. It was my first time in the area and I had no idea what to eat. We ended up at a small authentic Japanese restaurant where the menus were in a foreign language. Our group of friends was confused and had no idea how we were going to figure out what to order. The waitress saw our confusion and brought over a binder with photos of their dishes. This helped a bit but now had to wait for the binder to make its way around the table. I was hungry and getting anxious.
